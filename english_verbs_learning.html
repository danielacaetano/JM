Unit 6: Simple Present Verbs - Game Changer Starter

❤️ Love

I love, You love, He/She/It loves, We love, You love, They love

I love chocolate.
She loves reading books.

💔 Hate

I hate, You hate, He/She/It hates, We hate, You hate, They hate

I hate spiders.
They hate waking up early.

👍 Like

I like, You like, He/She/It likes, We like, You like, They like

We like playing football.
He likes ice cream.

👎 Don't Like

I don't like, You don't like, He/She/It doesn't like, We don't like, You don't like, They don't like

I don't like broccoli.
She doesn't like loud music.
